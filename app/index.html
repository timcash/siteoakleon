Software to build on

End to end field service software

Customer management

Store and full text search all of your customer details with flexible record formats.

Scheduling

Set up recurring appointments and have SMS or emails automatically send for confirmmation.

Routing

Automatically generated routes to reduce fuel cost and time spent driving. Routes adapt to new scheduling information.

Sales

Let your sales team operate using mobile devices and see how their teamates are performing.

Analytics

Create a living dashboard that is always up to date with the information you need to keep your business running. All data is easily exportable in a number of popular formats.

Auditing

Every change to the system is recorded and made available to users you give permissions. Rollback to previous versions of a record at the click of a button.

Payments

Take recurring credit card payments and see an account balance for each location. Let customers pay via their own page and view their history.

Fleet GPS

See your fleet in realtime with the ability to set geofences and speed limits. Get alerts based on the rules you define.

About Us

Jason Walton Co-founder

Jason leads the business development and marketing for Oakleon. Prior to co-founding Oakleon with Tim, he worked in the pest control industry. Since 1997, he founded 15 pest control companies with 22 branch locations in 8 different states. Jason's brands have included Moxie Pest Control, Homefront Pest Control, Joshua's Pest Control, and Mayday Pest Control. The companies founded and operated by him have combined value over $60 million in annual revenue. Prior to pest control ownership, he worked with Eclipse Marketing. Jason received a B.A. in Accounting from Brigham Young University.

Tim Cash Co-founder

Tim is leading the engineering effort at Oakleon. Before Oakleon he spent six years in the Air Force and ten years in the Intelligence Community heading up analytic, operational, and development teams. He has delieved a number of products ranging from web application used for national investigations to 3D visualisation software for geospatial analysis. While consulting for the government he pushed for more open access to information and expanded use of open source software. Tim holds a degree in Applied Mathematics and (most) of another in Computer Science.
